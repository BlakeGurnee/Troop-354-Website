Welcome to Troop 354

Welcome to Boy Scout Troop 354, located in Jenison, MI. Our troop is dedicated to fostering leadership, teamwork, and outdoor skills in young scouts through a range of engaging activities and projects.

We meet weekly to plan and participate in various activities including camping, hiking, and community service. Our troop is also involved in special events and projects throughout the year that help us build skills and create lasting memories.

Our commitment to scouting extends beyond just activities; we focus on developing strong character and a sense of responsibility in each of our scouts.
