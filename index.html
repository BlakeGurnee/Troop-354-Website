Welcome to Troop 354

Welcome to Boy Scout Troop 354, located in Jenison, MI. Our troop is dedicated to fostering leadership, teamwork, and outdoor skills in young scouts through a range of engaging activities and projects.

We meet every Monday evening from 7:00 to 8:30 PM whenever Jenison Public Schools are in session. If the schools are closed for weather or other reasons, our meetings are automatically canceled unless we provide other notification.

We are proud members of the Michigan Crossroads Council. Our troop is led by Scoutmaster Paul Flessner and Committee Chairman Todd Hollen. Our members come from Jenison, Hudsonville, and Grandville in Ottawa County, Michigan.

Regular Schedule

  • TROOP MEETINGS: Every Monday school is in session, 7pm – 8:30pm at Georgetown United Methodist Church
  • PATROL LEADERS COUNCIL: First Monday of each month, 8pm (at Troop meeting)
  • ADULT COMMITTEE MEETS: Last Monday of each month, 7pm – 9pm at Georgetown United Methodist Church
  • BOARD OF REVIEW: Last Wednesday of every month, 7pm at Holy Redeemer Parish
