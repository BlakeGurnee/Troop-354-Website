About Us

We are Troop 354 from Jenison, MI, led by Scout Master Paul Flesner. Our mission is to instill leadership skills in our scouts through various activities and monthly campouts.

Mr. Flesner, as we call him, has been a dedicated scout master for years, guiding young boys into becoming responsible and skilled individuals.
