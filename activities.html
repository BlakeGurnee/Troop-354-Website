Activities

Our troop engages in a variety of activities designed to build skills, foster teamwork, and provide fun. Activities include monthly campouts, community service projects, and skill-building workshops.

Upcoming Events

  • Monthly Campout: Every second weekend of the month, location varies.
  • Community Service: Various projects throughout the year to support local initiatives.
  • Workshops: Skills-focused sessions held periodically to enhance scouting knowledge.
